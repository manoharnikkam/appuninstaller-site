Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 21, 2026

Summary

App Uninstaller does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data. Everything happens locally on your Mac.

1. Data We Collect

None. The Software does not:

  • Collect analytics or usage data
  • Track your behavior or app usage
  • Send any information to external servers
  • Access the internet (except for license validation via Paddle)

2. License Validation

The Software uses Paddle for license management. When you purchase or activate a license, Paddle processes your payment and validates your license key. Paddle's privacy policy applies to that transaction: paddle.com/legal/privacy.

We do not have access to your payment details (credit card number, billing address, etc.). Paddle handles all payment processing.

3. Local File Access

The Software scans your file system to find files related to applications you choose to uninstall. This scanning happens entirely on your Mac. No file paths, names, or contents are ever transmitted externally.

4. Third-Party Services

The only third-party service used is Paddle for payment processing and license management. No other third-party SDKs, analytics tools, or tracking services are included.

5. Children's Privacy

The Software is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

6. Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

7. Contact
